What are the benefit amounts? The base plan comes with $1.5 million USD in total coverage. \Outpatient benefits have a maximum of $5,000 (10% co-insurance with Standard plan, no co-insurance with other plans).The following benefits are included only in Premium and Premium Plus Maternity:Dental max $1,500,Vision max $500,Screening & vaccines max $500,Maternity max $7,500.These amounts are in USD per policy year.\
