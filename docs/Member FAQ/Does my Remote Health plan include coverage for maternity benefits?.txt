Does my Remote Health plan include coverage for maternity benefits? The Premium Plus Maternity plan includes a $7,500 maximum benefit for maternity coverage. This includes coverage for maternity checkups, pre and post-natal care, and the baby's birth. This benefit does include a 20% co-insurance. That means that for maternity claims, you'll cover 20% of each claim. For example, if your claim is $100, Remote Health will reimburse $80, and you'll pay $20.The Standard and Premium plans do not include coverage for maternity benefits.
